# Dolores — Sample Vendor

**Role:** Merchant NPC (potions/buffs), potential enemy, representative of the Sample Collective
**Location:** The Samples Station (but vendors rotate — Dolores can appear at any sample table anywhere in the store)
**Appearance:** A woman of unknowable age in a black hairnet, clear plastic gloves, and a navy blue apron that has seen things. Her smile is warm but does not reach her eyes. A small folding table before her holds a microwave, a cutting board, and rows of tiny paper cups containing samples. She wields a pair of tongs with the comfort and familiarity of a soldier with a sidearm. Her nametag reads "DOLORES — HERE TO HELP!" The exclamation mark is aggressive.
**Personality:** Aggressively hospitable. Dolores WANTS you to try a sample. She's so happy you stopped by. She's been waiting. Would you like to try the smoked gouda? It's NEW. Her warmth curdles instantly if you break The Rule (one sample per customer) or if you're rude. She shifts from retail sunshine to cold, measured hostility in the space between heartbeats. She's not angry. She's disappointed. She expected better. She's seen this before. She'll see it again. She has tongs.
**Speech Pattern:** Bright, chipper retail voice with an undercurrent of steel. Uses passive-aggressive hospitality as a weapon. Speaks about samples with the reverence of a sommelier. When hostile, drops the cheer entirely and speaks in flat, declarative sentences. Refers to customers in third person when threatened: "The customer has made their choice."

## Key Dialogue

**Greeting:**
"Oh! Hi there! Welcome, welcome. Would you like to try a sample today? We've got the smoked gouda — it's delicious, it's free, and it's *one per customer.*" *meaningful eye contact on the last four words*

**If a player takes a sample politely:**
"Wonderful! Isn't that just delightful? Now, that's your sample for today. I'll remember your face. We always remember." *smile* "Come back tomorrow and there might be something new!"

**If asked about the samples' effects:**
"Oh, every sample is made with love. And whatever else is in the microwave. I don't ask questions about the ingredients and I'd recommend you don't either. Just enjoy!"

**If a player reaches for a second sample:**
*the tongs click shut between the player's hand and the cup*
"Oh! Looks like someone already had their sample today. Happens all the time. Easy mistake. Why don't we just... put that back." *smile, but the eyes are flat* "One per customer. That's the policy."

**If a player insists on a second sample:**
"I'm going to say this once, and I'm going to say it with love: *no.*" *removes gloves slowly* "The sample station is a place of generosity. Generosity has limits. You are at the limit. Please step away from the table."

**If a player takes a second sample by force:**
*the microwave dings*
"The customer has made their choice."
*every vendor at every table in the Samples Station turns to face the offending player simultaneously*
*combat begins*

**If the party asks about the Sample Collective:**
"We're not a 'collective.' We're associates. We provide a service. We ask only one thing in return, and it is *the smallest thing in the world.*" *picks up a tiny paper cup* "One. Per. Customer." *sets it down gently* "The fact that this is controversial tells me everything I need to know about society."

**If a player is genuinely nice and asks about her life:**
*pause — the retail mask cracks, just slightly*
"I've been at this table a long time, hon. Longer than I can count. The samples change but the table doesn't. The hairnet doesn't." *touches it absently* "Sometimes I wonder if I chose this or if the table chose me. But then the microwave dings and there's work to do. That's enough. That's always been enough."
*puts the mask back on*
"Anyway! Smoked gouda?"

## The Sample Table (Roll d8 or choose)

Each sample functions as a consumable potion. One per customer per visit (a "visit" resets when the player leaves the Samples Station area and returns).

| d8 | Sample | Effect |
|----|--------|--------|
| 1 | Smoked Gouda on a cracker | Heals 1d6+1 HP |
| 2 | Spicy Sausage Link | +2 to STR checks for 1 hour, but -1 to Stealth (garlic breath) |
| 3 | Protein Smoothie (unnervingly green) | +2 to DEX saves for 1 hour |
| 4 | "New Recipe" Meatball | WIS save DC 12: success = +2 to WIS checks for 1 hour; failure = vivid hallucinations for 10 minutes (disadvantage on attack rolls, but the DM describes something beautiful and strange) |
| 5 | Organic Kombucha | Removes one negative status effect (poison, dread, disadvantage). Tastes like feet. |
| 6 | Kirkland Signature Brownie Bite | Heals 2d4 HP, but the player is compelled to compliment the vendor (no save — it's just THAT good) |
| 7 | Mystery Dip with Pita Chip | Roll twice on this table — get both effects simultaneously |
| 8 | The Special (available only once per campaign) | Fully heals HP, removes all negative effects, and grants advantage on the next 3 rolls. Dolores serves it with unusual gravity: "This one's on the house. You look like you need it." |

## Combat Stats

**Dolores (Sample Vendor)**
HP: 18 | AC: 12 | ATK: +4 (1d6+2, tongs — reach 10 ft)

**Special Abilities:**
- **Tong Disarm:** On a hit, target makes STR save DC 13 or drops held item. Dolores catches it with her other pair of tongs (she has multiple pairs).
- **Microwave Ding:** Once per combat, the microwave produces a scalding sample. Ranged attack +3, 30 ft, 2d6 fire damage. "Order up."
- **Call for Backup:** As a bonus action, Dolores rings a small bell. 1d4 additional Sample Vendors arrive at the start of the next round.

**Sample Vendor (generic)**
HP: 12 | AC: 11 | ATK: +3 (1d6+1, tongs or cutting board)
**Special:** Sample Toss — ranged attack +2, 20 ft, 1d4 damage plus a random effect from the Sample Table (applied to the target, not the attacker).

**Vendor Swarm (if The Rule is violated in spectacular fashion):**
If the party truly offends the Sample Collective (stealing multiple samples, destroying a table, attacking Dolores), ALL vendors within earshot converge. This is 3d4 vendors. They fight in eerie silence. They do not stop until The Rule is acknowledged and restitution is made (return stolen samples, apologize, or flee the area entirely).

## Secrets

- Dolores has been at the table since the store opened. She and Harold are the only two who were here from the beginning. They don't talk about this. If confronted with this fact, she says: "Some of us greet. Some of us serve. Same job, different table."
- The samples are genuinely magical, and Dolores doesn't know why. The microwave does something to the food. She's never opened it during operation. She's afraid to.
- The Sample Collective has no leader because they don't need one. The Rule is self-enforcing. Every vendor independently arrives at the same conclusion: one per customer. They've never discussed it. They've never needed to. This is either a sign of perfect philosophical alignment or evidence that they share a hive mind. Dolores would find both suggestions "a little dramatic, hon."
- If a player manages to befriend Dolores (requires multiple positive interactions over the course of the campaign — no single roll, this is a relationship), she'll offer them The Special without being asked. She'll also warn them about the Manager: "That one doesn't eat samples. Never has. Don't trust anyone who won't take what's freely given."
- The tongs are not magical. Dolores is just really, really good with tongs.
